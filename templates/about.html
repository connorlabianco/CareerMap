No matter who you are, you probably agree; learning new things can be hard. Whether it is time management, difficult concepts, or lack of interest, there are many hurdles to overcome when learning a new skill.

Our mission when creating CareerMap was to make this process easier and more fun. Our program will take job postings with skills you want to learn, and spoon-feed these skills over multiple days.

This works directly in your Google Calendar, and will work around your schedule to optimize the time you can spend on your goals.

Learning can be hard, but it doesn't have to be. Make learning fun with CareerMap.
